From Garden to Plate

A potager is afrench term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planed with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to plant

Plants are chosen as much for thier functionality as for thier color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-desgined potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintinance. Potagers can disgusie their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formatlity of a knot garden
